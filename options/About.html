About

The Archiver is an attempt to make it easier for people to preserve and browse the Internet of old. Its imperative that we preserve the Legacy of the web as much as possible.

The internet is changing at an alarming rate, pages are being lost, forgotten and defaced so please use this tool when you can to save every page you deem worthy.

You never know when you might need the information again and you will be helping countless others along the way.

Thank you
